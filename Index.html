What's the story? About our company

As kids, sometimes it's hard for nerdy girls to find other girls with similar interest, discouraging their passion for comics and the media industry on the whole. As adults, we started this company to prevent that from happening

What do you do? About our solution

We created the first-ever free, virtual, and family-friendly comic convention for girls! We hope that the event encorages all who attend to keep being themselves and developing their unique interests.

What is it like? About our convention

After you register, you'll be able to login to our site and access the registrant-specific conference video call links. Once on-call, you'll be able to participate in our full schedule of workshops, contests, and panels.
